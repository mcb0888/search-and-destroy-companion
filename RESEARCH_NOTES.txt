CALLOUT RESEARCH BASIS — v0.8

Raid
- Activision Raid Map Snapshot: official landmarks and three-lane layout.
- Long-running competitive Raid terminology: Water, Water Steps, Kitchen, Money, Mid, Zig, Pillars, Ring, Laundry, Driveway, Art, Garage, Lambo, White Truck, Tiki, Basketball.
- CODM community discussions confirm these names remain recognizable, with minor squad variation.

Tunisia
- Activision Tunisia Map Snapshot: Main Square, Center Street, south second-story Window, Scaffolding, west Stairs, Ladder and Platform.
- CODM community usage adds short forms such as Square, Scaffold, Window, Center, West, Alley and Zig.

Coastal
- Coastal is a Search & Destroy-focused CODM map with broad outdoor lanes and indoor routes.
- Community/strategy discussion repeatedly uses Winery, A Courtyard, Ladder, B Site/Rooms, Mid, Arch, Alley, Back and elevated/upper callouts.
- Coastal naming is less standardized than Raid, so the app shows aliases and favors obvious landmarks.

Method
- Facts and landmarks were summarized in original wording.
- No official map artwork, screenshots, or copied diagrams are included.
- The app presents a practical common vocabulary, not a claim that every squad uses identical names.
