VISUAL AND RESEARCH NOTES

All maps displayed by this build are locally packaged tactical visuals created for the Search & Destroy Companion. They are designed for rapid recognition and tactical explanation rather than serving as official map artwork or exact architectural survey drawings.

Map structure, landmark naming, and general tactical orientation were researched using official Activision map overviews, the official 2026 CODM esports settings, competitive references, community callout usage, and gameplay-oriented map references.
